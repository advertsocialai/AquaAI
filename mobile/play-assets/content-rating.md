# Play Console — Content Rating (IARC) questionnaire answers

Reference doc for filling in the Play Console "App content" → "Content rating" questionnaire.

## Category
**Utility, Productivity, Communication, or Other**

(Aqua Rudra is a productivity / utility app for farmers — pick this category, not Games.)

---

## IARC questions

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Does your app contain violence? | **No** |
| Does your app contain sexuality or nudity? | **No** |
| Does your app contain language or content that may not be suitable for all ages (profanity, crude humour, drugs, alcohol, tobacco)? | **No** |
| Does your app contain simulated gambling? | **No** |
| Does your app allow users to interact, communicate or share information with other users? | **Yes — text messaging (support chat) only** |
| Does your app share users' physical location with other users? | **No** |
| Does your app allow users to purchase digital goods? | **No** (currently no in-app purchases; if billing is added later, update this answer) |
| Does your app contain user-generated content visible to other users? | **No** (photos you upload are visible only to you and authorised processors, e.g. lab partners you explicitly share a certificate with) |

## Expected rating

Based on these answers, IARC will rate the app **3+** (all ages) or **PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone** equivalents.

---

## Target audience and content

Play Console asks separately:

- **Primary target audience** → **17 and over** (we recommend 16+ because users handle KYC and certificate generation).
- **Does your app appeal to children?** → **No**
- **Does your app contain ads?** → **No**
