## Full description (≤4000 chars)

AquaRudra is an aquaculture intelligence app built for India's shrimp and fish farmers, hatcheries, processors, and village-level entrepreneurs (VLEs). Run on-device AI diagnostics, get water-quality alerts, calculate feed and stocking, browse the input marketplace, and generate verifiable digital certificates — all in 6 Indian languages, online or offline.

**What you can do**

▸ Disease Detector — Snap a photo of your shrimp, prawn or fish and get an instant AI-flagged condition assessment with suggested next steps.

▸ Seed Counter — Point your camera at a tray and count post-larvae or fry in seconds. Saves the count to your farm log.

▸ Water Quality Grader — Capture a sample and grade pH, turbidity and visible condition; get plain-language thresholds for your species and life stage.

▸ Weather & Rain Alarm — 7-day forecast and instant alerts when heavy rain risks pond overflow or dissolved-oxygen crashes.

▸ Certificates & Verification — Generate QR-stamped quality / lineage certificates for outgoing batches. Anyone can scan to verify authenticity on aquaai.in.

▸ Marketplace — Browse seed suppliers, feed brands, equipment dealers and lab services near you.

▸ Advisory & Calculators — Stocking density, FCR, biofloc, pond preparation, harvest planning — built for Indian conditions, not generic tropical advice.

▸ Knowledge Hub — Practical articles on disease prevention, biosecurity, post-harvest, export compliance and government schemes.

**Built for India**

▸ Works in 6 languages: English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Odia, Bengali.

▸ Voice reader on every article — listen while you work.

▸ Offline-first — diagnostics, articles, calculators and farm logs work without internet. Syncs when you reconnect.

▸ Designed for entry-level Android phones (Android 8.0+), low data usage.

**Who is this for**

▸ Shrimp, prawn and finfish farmers running ponds, cages or RAS systems.

▸ Hatchery operators who need defensible PL counts and quality grades.

▸ Processors and exporters who need verifiable certificates of lineage and quality.

▸ Extension officers, VLEs, NGOs and university students working with coastal aquaculture communities.
