# Demo video script · 90 seconds

For partner meetings and the home-page hero. Voice-over in Telugu first,
English subtitled. Music: low ambient, no vocals.

## Scene 1 · 0:00 – 0:08 · Hook

VO: "Indian farmers lose ₹12,000 crore every year — because the data
they need doesn't reach them in time."

VISUAL: cold open of a flooded pond at dawn, then a farmer staring
at a WhatsApp message on a basic Android.

## Scene 2 · 0:08 – 0:22 · Diagnose

VO: "30 seconds. A photo on a ₹500 USB microscope tells him if his
PLs are EHP-positive."

VISUAL: screen-cap of the AquaI mobile app. Tray → 3-frame burst →
EHP CLEAR badge with confidence 94%.

## Scene 3 · 0:22 – 0:38 · Price

VO: "Live mandi rates from every district. He sells when the price
peaks, not when the buyer decides."

VISUAL: scrolling price ticker on the web dashboard, with a green
"+₹12" delta on Vannamei 40-count.

## Scene 4 · 0:38 – 0:52 · Verify

VO: "An MPEDA-aligned QC certificate. Signed. QR-verifiable. A bank
will lend against this."

VISUAL: PDF certificate slides into view, QR code zooms in, hands
scanning it with a phone.

## Scene 5 · 0:52 – 1:08 · Network

VO: "Hatcheries. Transporters. Insurers. The whole supply chain in
one place — in your language."

VISUAL: split-screen carousel of hatchery dashboard, transporter
load board, bank risk-scoring panel. Language switcher cycles through
Telugu → Tamil → Hindi → Odia → Bengali.

## Scene 6 · 1:08 – 1:25 · Government scale

VO: "MPEDA gets a live outbreak map. NSPAAD gets surveillance data
in real time. India gets ahead of the next outbreak."

VISUAL: surveillance map of coastal India with pulsing severity dots.

## Scene 7 · 1:25 – 1:30 · Close

VO: "AquaI. Decoding aquaculture, one pond at a time."

VISUAL: logo + tagline + URL `aquai.in`.

---

## B-roll shot list

- Pond at dawn / dusk
- Farmer holding a basic Android phone (Redmi Note baseline)
- USB pen microscope with LED ring
- White acrylic counting tray + PLs
- VLE in a polo shirt with a tablet, talking to a farmer
- Hatchery floor with paddle wheels running
- Truck loading at sunset (ice + reefer)
- MPEDA officer at a desk reviewing a map
