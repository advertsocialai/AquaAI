# Partner onboarding playbook

Step-by-step for each partner archetype. Owner is named per row;
durations are working days.

## A · Hatchery

| Step | Owner            | Days | Output                                  |
|------|------------------|------|-----------------------------------------|
| 1    | BD               | 1    | Initial call · MPEDA license collected  |
| 2    | Compliance       | 2    | License verified, GST + bank linked     |
| 3    | Field engineer   | 1    | On-site visit · facility photos         |
| 4    | Onboarding       | 2    | Staff training · 2 VLE + 1 manager      |
| 5    | Engineering      | 1    | B2B portal access + API tokens issued   |
| 6    | BD               | 7    | First 5 batches certified, ratings live |

Activation milestone: 5 signed QC certs in 14 days. Below this → BD follow-up call.

## B · Farmer (via VLE cluster)

| Step | Owner           | Days | Output                                   |
|------|-----------------|------|------------------------------------------|
| 1    | VLE             | 1    | Doorstep visit · app installed           |
| 2    | VLE             | 1    | KYC walkthrough · Aadhaar + bank verified|
| 3    | VLE             | 1    | First QC session · cert delivered        |
| 4    | Farmer          | 0    | First mandi-price alert subscribed        |
| 5    | Engineering     | 0    | Crop calendar generated based on stocking|

Activation milestone: first cert + first alert in 7 days.

## C · Bank / Insurer

| Step | Owner             | Days | Output                                     |
|------|-------------------|------|--------------------------------------------|
